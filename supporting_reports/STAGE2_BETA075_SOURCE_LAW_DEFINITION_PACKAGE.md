## Interpretation

This is the first source-law package that can be read as more than an effective
numerical device. It states what the source law is, which known source families
are too small, which family is the current lead, and where hyperbolicity and
exchange evidence enter the claim.

The result does not claim a final matter action. It does show that the observed
law lives near recognizable physical structures: anisotropic heat-current
media, radial director/aether-like variables, elastic/support reservoirs, and
open-system effective actions. That is a meaningful move from "does the math
work?" toward "can a known physical source family realize this?"
