## Interpretation

The refinement does not expose a hard physics wall. A hard wall would have
shown up as failed real characteristics, cone escape, live support leakage,
angular support leakage, or an inability to restore total conservation through
the explicit support sector. None of those happened.

It also does not solve the margin problem. The recomputed principal symbol
lands on the same dense minimum margin, `7.881e-05`, which means the thin cone
margin is primarily the heat-current/transport edge of the observed
source-family state, not an artifact of leaving the support reservoir vague.
The support-reservoir operator is acceptable as a fixed-background derivative
operator, but it does not create new hyperbolic cushion by itself.

The local `P` issue remains the clearest physical refinement target. The worst
power-channel shape is reset-decompression/core throat at `0.609031`; the
worst radial-force channel is reset-decompression/support edge at `0.475062`.
Because aggregate P/F exchange and total support conservation pass, this is a
derivation and safety-margin watch rather than a new-component demand. The
support reservoir needs a more explicit stress-potential / stroke-history
derivation, especially for reset-decompression power transfer.

## Current Read
