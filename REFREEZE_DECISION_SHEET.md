# Refreeze Decision Sheet

**Promote:** R1.75 shaped catch radial-soft lapse-cushion v1.

**Validation status:** packet-safe at V=2, V=5, and V=10 in the tested reduced model; not robust above V=10.

**Primary design lessons:**

- Tkk wants shaped locked-lead catch timing.
- p_l wants radial support-edge widening.
- Packet safety wants a lapse cushion.
- The catch/rematch residual wants a source-side modal packet-edge absorber.
- Uniform radial widening has a sharp safety cliff; future headroom likely requires nonuniform radial support shaping.

**Source-proxy finish-line:** passenger-facing catch/rematch Tkk underfit was reduced below 5% by a modal catch-edge absorber. That supports a reduced source-proxy success claim, not a physical source construction claim.
